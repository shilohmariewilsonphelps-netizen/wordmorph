Welcome to WordMorph (Beta)

What this is:
WordMorph is a word laboratory for exploring how words evolve through prefixes, suffixes, and creative combinations.

How to use it:

  1. Enter a base word such as love, hope, care, or fear
  2. Select the prefixes, suffixes, or categories you want to test
  3. Click Generate Words
  4. Click any generated word to explore its structure and meaning

Beta notes:

  • Some definitions are still being refined
  • Spelling rules are improving but not perfect
  • All generated words are shown, including unusual ones, to support experimentation
